## Rate Limiting Details

Rate limiting helps ensure fair usage and system stability. Limits are applied per application.

**Starter Plan:**
- 50 messages per hour per application
- Suitable for low to moderate traffic
- Example: Small e-commerce site with occasional customer questions

**Professional Plan:**
- 200 messages per hour per application
- Suitable for moderate to high traffic
- Example: Growing SaaS product with active user base

**Enterprise Plan:**
- Custom rate limits (typically 500-1,000 messages/hour per app)
- Can be adjusted based on needs
- Example: High-traffic e-commerce during sales events

**Note:** Rate limits can be temporarily increased for special events. Contact support for assistance.

## Visitor Limits

Monthly visitor limits are based on unique visitors who interact with your chat widget.

**What counts as a visitor:**
- A unique user who opens or interacts with your chat widget
- Counted once per month, even if they return multiple times
- Visitors who only view the widget but don't interact are not counted

**What happens if you exceed limits:**
- Starter & Professional: Chat continues to work, but you'll be notified to upgrade
- Enterprise: No limits, chat always available

## Application Limits

Each application is a separate chat widget instance with its own configuration.

**Starter (1 application):**
- One chat widget for your main website
- Example: `yourcompany.deliverychat.com`

**Professional (5 applications):**
- Multiple chat widgets for different products or regions
- Example: `product1.deliverychat.com`, `product2.deliverychat.com`, `us.deliverychat.com`, `eu.deliverychat.com`, `support.deliverychat.com`

**Enterprise (Unlimited):**
- As many applications as you need
- Perfect for large organizations with multiple brands, products, or regions

## Upgrade & Downgrade

**Upgrading:**
- Upgrade anytime from your dashboard
- Changes take effect immediately
- Prorated billing for the current month

**Downgrading:**
- Downgrade at the end of your billing cycle
- Your data is preserved
- Features beyond your new plan are disabled but data remains accessible

**Free Trial:**
- 14 days full access to Professional plan features
- No credit card required
- Cancel anytime during trial

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I change plans later?**
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes are prorated.

**What happens if I exceed my visitor limit?**
Your chat continues to work. You'll receive notifications to upgrade. No service interruption.

**Do unused visitors roll over?**
No, visitor limits reset monthly. Consider upgrading if you consistently exceed limits.

**Can I have custom rate limits?**
Enterprise plans include custom rate limiting. Professional plans can request temporary increases for events.

**Is there a setup fee?**
No setup fees. Only monthly subscription costs.

**What payment methods do you accept?**
Credit cards, debit cards, and for Enterprise plans, we can arrange invoicing.

**Can I get a refund?**
Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans.
