## Rate Limiting Details

Rate limiting helps ensure fair usage and system stability. Limits are applied per organization (tenant) across all API requests (REST, SDK, widget).

**Free Plan:**
- 5 requests/sec, 50/min, 500/hour
- Suitable for testing and very low traffic
- Example: Personal blog or portfolio site

**Basic Plan:**
- 10 requests/sec, 100/min, 1,000/hour
- Suitable for low to moderate traffic
- Example: Small e-commerce site with occasional customer questions

**Premium Plan:**
- 25 requests/sec, 500/min, 5,000/hour
- Suitable for moderate to high traffic
- Example: Growing SaaS product with active user base

**Enterprise Plan:**
- Default: 50 requests/sec, 1,000/min, 10,000/hour
- Custom limits configurable via admin dashboard
- Example: High-traffic e-commerce during sales events

<Callout type="note">
  Enterprise plans can configure custom rate limits in Settings → Rate Limits. Premium plans can request temporary increases for special events.
</Callout>

## Visitor Limits

Monthly visitor limits are based on unique visitors who interact with your chat widget.

**What counts as a visitor:**
- A unique user who opens or interacts with your chat widget
- Counted once per month, even if they return multiple times
- Visitors who only view the widget but don't interact are not counted

**What happens if you exceed limits:**
- Free, Basic & Premium: Chat continues to work, but you'll be notified to upgrade
- Enterprise: No limits, chat always available

## Application Limits

Each application is a separate chat widget instance with its own configuration. An application represents a chat widget that can be embedded on a specific website domain. Your organization gets one subdomain (e.g., `yourcompany.deliverychat.com`) that serves all your applications.

**Free & Basic (1 application):**
- One chat widget for your main website
- Example: Embed widget on `yourcompany.com` (connects to `yourcompany.deliverychat.com`)

**Premium (5 applications):**
- Multiple chat widgets for different products, brands, or regions
- Example: Embed widgets on `product1.com`, `product2.com`, `us.yourcompany.com`, `eu.yourcompany.com`, `support.yourcompany.com` (all connect to `yourcompany.deliverychat.com`)

**Enterprise (Unlimited):**
- As many applications as you need
- Perfect for large organizations with multiple brands, products, or regions
- Each application can be embedded on a different website domain

## Upgrade & Downgrade

**Upgrading:**
- Upgrade anytime from your dashboard
- Changes take effect immediately
- Prorated billing for the current month

**Downgrading:**
- Downgrade at the end of your billing cycle
- Your data is preserved
- Features beyond your new plan are disabled but data remains accessible

**Free Trial:**
- 14 days full access to all paid plan features
- No credit card required
- Cancel anytime during trial
